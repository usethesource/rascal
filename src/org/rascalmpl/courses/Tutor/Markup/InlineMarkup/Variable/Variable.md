# Variable

.Synopsis
Markup for a variable.

.Syntax

* `\VarName`
* `\VarName\~Index~`
* `\VarName\^Index^`


.Types

.Function

.Details

.Description
Variables in text and code are represented by ((Italic)) markup. 
They may be followed by one or more subscripts (enclosed by `~` and `~`) or superscripts (enclosed by `^` and `^`)

.Examples

* `\Var` gives _Var_.

* `\Var\~1~` gives _Var_~1~.

* `\Var\^1^` gives _Var_^1^.

* `\Var\^1^\~2~` gives _Var_^1^~2~.
